A research intern at IIIT Delhi under prof. Dr Rajiv Ratn Shah. My work at IIIT Delhi includes extensive research on information retrieval from different aspects of social media as well as on the multimodal analysis of social media. Multimodal analysis of social media is a bigger aspect of the information retrieval from social media which covers work such as analysis of social media through different modalities(like text, emoji, speech and video). I have done a considerable amount of research in the field of information retrieval from multilingual texts and have won global rank of 4 during my internship in the FIRE’18 task. I also published my own research paper in the field of information retrieval titled “author profiling from multilingual SMS”.
