CloudCV

Working with CloudCV on their Fabrik project

Fabrik is an online collaborative platform to build, visualize and train deep learning models via a simple drag-and-drop interface. It allows researchers to collaboratively develop and debug models using a web GUI that supports importing, editing and exporting networks written in widely popular frameworks like Caffe, Keras, and TensorFlow. Such an amazing platform comes with the heavy cost of developers putting their mind and time into it. Contributing to their platform is itself an amazing task which in return is definitely increasing my knowledge and my love for open source.
